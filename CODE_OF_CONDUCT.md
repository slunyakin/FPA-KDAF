# Code of Conduct

KDAF follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about risks and trade-offs.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks.
- Public disclosure of private security issues.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior is harmful to the project community.
